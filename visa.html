[Getting A Visa]

Kenyan eVisa is issued for various purposes such as tourism, recreation, sightseeing, casual visit to meet friends or relatives, short duration medical treatment or casual business visit, whereby traveling to Kenya has not ever been as easy as now with this appearance. Besides, the foreign nationals wishing to travel to Kenya are required to possess a valid passport of their country and a valid Kenyan Visa. In line with that trend, we supply the online professional service for Kenyan eVisa application within 3 easy steps.

1. Fill out the online application form

Fill out the online application form by providing us your flight information including the airport of arrival, the date of arrival, and your personal information such as your full name, the passport number, date of birth and nationality Your personal information supplied and the information on your passport must match together.

2. Pay the service fee and Government fee online

Pay the service fee and Government fee online by your Credit/Debit Card or Paypal mentioned in Payment Guidelines: After finishing the payment, we will send you an email for confirming your eVisa order and documents request to complete the Kenyan eVisa application. Then we will proceed and send it to you by email. Please check requirements for more information.

3. Print out Kenyan eVisa.

Print out Kenyan eVisa. After that, please present your eVisa for checking at the airports in Kenya. It takes you only 5-10 minutes to get the stamp on your passport by Kenyan Immigration Officers at the airports. The Kenyan eVisa will make you confident to travel to Kenya and through us, your trip will be more convenient.
